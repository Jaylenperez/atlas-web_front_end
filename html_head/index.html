Why I chose Atlas Full Stack

For a long time, I struggled to find a career path that truly resonated with me. After high school, I attended community college, following a familiar path without a clear direction. However, after facing a difficult personal loss, I found myself reevaluating my priorities. I decided to take a step back, focus on my well-being, and explore my interests. During this period of self-reflection, a family friend introduced me to Atlas (formerly Holberton). Inspired by their success and eager to find my own passion, I enrolled in the program. Although the journey has been challenging, it has also been incredibly rewarding, leading me to discover a deep interest in programming.
